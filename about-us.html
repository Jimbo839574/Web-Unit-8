Venera can be found on Westminster Street in the heart of London, offering clients a refined shopping experience in one of the city’s most prestigious areas. Our flagship store is conveniently located at 27 Westminster Street, London, SW1A 2AB, providing easy access for both local and international customers. Visitors are welcomed into an elegant space where luxury clothing, vintage-inspired collections, and bespoke tailoring services come together in a setting that reflects Venera’s heritage and commitment to excellence.

Venera offers luxury clothing that reflects timeless elegance and superior craftsmanship. Specializing in vintage-inspired designs, each piece is carefully created using high-quality materials and refined tailoring techniques. In addition to its ready-to-wear collections, Venera provides bespoke tailoring services, allowing clients to enjoy garments tailored precisely to their individual style and fit. This commitment to detail, quality, and personalization ensures a sophisticated experience that sets Venera apart in the luxury fashion industry.

Founded in 1850, Venera has been proudly operating for over 170 years, establishing a long-standing reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and timeless design. Throughout its history, the company has remained committed to quality, tradition, and attention to detail. With three stores worldwide and a dedicated team of 16 experienced professionals, Venera continues to offer vintage-style clothing and bespoke tailoring services, blending heritage techniques with personalized customer care.
